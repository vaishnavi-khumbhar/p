Our Culinary Journey

At Sangram Resort, we believe food is more than just a meal – it’s an experience that tells the story of tradition, culture, and heartfelt hospitality.

Nestled in the heart of Kolhapur, our resort celebrates the bold flavors and rich heritage of local cuisine, blending them with modern culinary techniques. Every dish is prepared with fresh, handpicked ingredients and served with warmth, ensuring that every guest feels at home. Whether you’re savoring our signature Kolhapuri dishes or exploring global delicacies, we promise an unforgettable dining experience that lingers beyond the table.
